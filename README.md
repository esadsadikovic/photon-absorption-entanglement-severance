## Abstract
Dark matter and dark energy together constitute ~95% of the universe’s energy budget, yet neither has a confirmed origin. This paper proposes a unified mechanism: photon absorption as entanglement severance. Within Resonance Cosmology, photon emission produces a momentum-entangled state with its source; absorption severs this correlation through gravitational interaction, releasing a latent dark contribution not captured by standard stress-energy accounting. 

The severance mechanism is density-dependent: in dense media, severed states localise into pressureless dust (w = 0, dark matter); in diffuse media, they couple to vacuum field modes (w = -1, dark energy). This reframing dissolves the cosmological constant problem by distinguishing between E1-band vacuum energy and the accumulated ether density from diffuse photon absorption. Six falsifiable predictions are presented, grounding the mechanism in observational cosmology.
